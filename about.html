Our Story

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight. Initially operating as a distributor for Japanese shoe maker Onitsuka Tiger (now ASICS), Nike grew rapidly and became one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel. The company's iconic swoosh logo and "Just Do It" slogan are globally recognized symbols of athletic excellence and motivation.
